Have you noticed that summers are hotter and winters are warmer?

Do you ever see breaking news about an extreme weather event that threatens the places people call home?

Well, these have events happen because of the rapid climate change occurring on Earth.

People sometimes ask, “What’s the difference between global warming and climate change?”

Well, global warming refers to the long term warming of our planet while climate change refers to the changes that are happening to our planet such as a change in weather patterns and extreme weather events.
