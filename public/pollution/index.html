POLLUTION

Pollution is the introduction of harmful substances and/or contaminants into the environment.

Pollution, the neccessary evil that comes with developing societies.

It comes in many forms.

Land, Air, Water, Light, Noise, etc.

Let's explore how these specific types affect our planet.

POLLUTION

Land Pollution

Lands and natural environments affected by the introduction of landfills being filled with both Household and Industrial waste.

According to the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA), We americans produce about 4.3 pounds of waster per person per day.

POLLUTION

Air Pollution

Occurs when dangerous gases and chemical vapors are introduced into the atmosphere through the burning of fossil fuels, smoke and smog as well as greenhouse gases.

POLLUTION

Water Pollution

Caused by harmful chemicals and substances such as sewage, pesticides and fertilizers being introduced to water.

POLLUTION

Light Pollution

The excessive use of artificial light affects not just our views of the nighttime sky but can also affect us, the wildlife and climate of our planet.

POLLUTION

No matter what type of pollution, both humans and wildlife are constantly being affected.
